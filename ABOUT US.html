About Us

J&P Pares and Mami

J & P Pares is a business that offers pares and mami, located on Earnshaw St. near Honorio Lopez Bridge. It was established on June 9, 2023 in order to be an additional source of income to the owner, Pennylane Tindugan Dalisay. We offer an establishment with a homely atmosphere and food that will ease the stomach, offering unli-rice in accompaniment so customers can eat to their heart's content.
